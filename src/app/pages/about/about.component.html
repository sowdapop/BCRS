About Us

Bob's Computer Repair Shop is a family-owned and operated business that has been serving the local community for over 10 years. Our goal is to provide affordable, high-quality computer repair services to homes and small businesses in the area.



At Bob's Computer Repair Shop, we offer a wide range of services including virus removal, data recovery, hardware upgrades, software installations, and much more. Our team of experts can diagnose and repair almost any issue, big or small. We use only the highest-quality parts and materials, and we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty.

We believe in providing a personal touch to our services. We take the time to understand our clients' needs and provide customized solutions that meet their specific requirements. Our team of experts is available to answer any questions and provide support both during and after the repair process.
